Flagship Projects & Systems
Training & Quality Tools Ecosystem
Product‑style T&Q

Over the years I have developed an ecosystem of training and quality tools: PowerApps forms, HTML templates, SharePoint lists and automated flows that keep QA, Training and Ops aligned. These tools make coaching, performance insights and reminders consistent and accessible to everyone. For example:

  • Coaching & reminder cards with branded HTML templates for easy, actionable feedback.
  • Performance vs. potential mapping tools to visualize who needs support and who is ready for growth.
  • Case randomizer to select QA samples fairly and transparently.
  • Previewers and converters that turn HTML into images for decks, emails and PowerApps.

DSAT / QA Analytics & Storytelling
KANO · Roadmaps

I regularly take raw DSAT/QA exports (WB weeks, tenure buckets, drivers) and transform them into executive-friendly stories: KANO categorizations, Pareto charts, bottom/top quartile views and 30-60-90 plans that make it clear what we’ll do next.

This includes slicing by tenure, channel, driver and AOO; mapping surveys to “Must Have / Performance / Delighter”; and suggesting targeted refreshers for things like unresponsive behavior, soft skills and mis-positioned policies.
